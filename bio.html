Biography

Working as render programmer @ Ubisoft Belgrade During the day I work on programming 3D computer graphics for AAA games, while at night I work as digital artist in domain of new media and computer art. Mostly I've been inspired by 90s demo scene and new media movements.

I am using computers to create interactive computing and visual interfaces which try to convey certain message while being aesthetically pleasing.

- SYNOPSIS

tools [ openFrameworks | Max/MSP | TouchDesigner | Unreal Engine ]
medium [ computer graphics | digital signals | audio input | depth cameras]
motives [ accelerationism | aesthetics | hacking culture ]

- DESCRIPTION

My biggest struggle is trying to put skills of making pixels on screens flash, dance, move and react to something that is beyond digital. At least that's how I wish to percieve it. That moment of interaction between worlds we don't see [air (full of magnetic signals and networks), internet (immensly high bandwith information highway)] and something in front of our face (screen).

I've worked with artists, held presentations in domain of digital computer art, did live visual sets and provided wide variety of visual solutions.
